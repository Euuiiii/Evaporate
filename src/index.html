Evaporate

Evaporate is a portable utility that completely erases your digital footprint from this computer.

  1. Run the App: Double-click the executable directly from your USB drive.
  2. Evaporate Session: Click the main button to close browsers, wipe cookies, and shred temporary files.
  3. Unmount & Go: Wait for the success message, unplug your USB, and walk away with total peace of mind.
